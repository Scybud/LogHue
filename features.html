Features That Keep Teams Organized and Moving

LogHue is built for teams that need clarity, structure, and accountability, without the noise of bloated tools. Whether you're coordinating a project or documenting your work, LogHue keeps everything clean and predictable.

Core Workspace Features

Team‑Ready Workspaces

Each workspace is a dedicated environment for your team. Tasks, notes, activity, and members all live in one structured space, making collaboration clear and predictable.

Task Management

Create tasks, assign owners, update details, and track progress. Admins manage structure; members update their own work. Every change is logged for transparency.

Activity Timeline

Every workspace includes a chronological activity feed. See who updated what, when, and why, without digging through messages or guessing what changed.

LogHue Notes

A clean, minimal writing space for documentation, meeting notes, and internal knowledge. Export support is coming soon, making it easy to share or archive your work.

Role‑Based Access

Admins manage workspace structure and settings. Members update tasks and contribute content. Guests can view or participate depending on permissions. Simple, predictable, and secure.

Real‑Time Sync

Powered by Supabase’s real‑time engine, updates appear instantly across all devices. No refreshes, no delays — your team always sees the latest state.

Personal Tools Inside LogHue

While LogHue is team‑first, it includes personal tools that help individuals stay organized and reflect on their work, even outside a team environment.

Personal Task Logger

Log completed tasks, add notes, and track your personal progress over time. Perfect for self‑review, accountability, and building a history of your work.

Personal Notes

Use LogHue Notes as a lightweight personal documentation tool. Draft ideas, write study notes, or keep reference material, all in a clean, distraction‑free editor.

Built for Real Workflows

Teams & Organizations

LogHue is designed for teams that need structure. Plan work, track progress, document decisions, and keep everyone aligned. Workspaces provide clarity without unnecessary complexity.

Independent Creators

Even though LogHue is team‑oriented, solo creators benefit from the personal task logger and notes system. Organize your work, track your progress, and keep your ideas structured.

Students & Learners

Use workspaces for group projects or rely on personal notes and the task logger for individual study. LogHue helps you stay organized without overwhelming you.

Developers & Technical Teams

Track issues, document architecture, and maintain clarity across technical work. LogHue offers a lightweight, structured alternative to heavy project‑management tools.

Under the Hood

Supabase‑Powered Backend

Authentication, database, storage, and real‑time sync, all powered by a modern, scalable backend built for reliability and speed.

Secure by Design

Role‑based permissions, secure storage, and GDPR‑aligned data handling ensure your team’s work stays safe and compliant.

Modular Architecture

LogHue is built from isolated modules that scale cleanly. This keeps the interface predictable and prevents features from interfering with each other.

Lightweight, Predictable Interface

LogHue is designed to stay fast and consistent. Every workspace loads cleanly, updates instantly, and keeps your structure intact — without unnecessary animations, clutter, or distractions.
