Our Pricing Philosophy

LogHue is built for teams that value clarity, structure, and simplicity. Our pricing reflects that philosophy, no hidden fees, no confusing add‑ons, and no artificial limitations designed to force upgrades.

The Free plan gives individuals and small groups everything they need to get started. The Pro plan supports growing groups with more structure and collaboration. And Team unlocks full flexibility for teams that rely on LogHue every day.

We believe great tools should scale with you, not get in your way.
