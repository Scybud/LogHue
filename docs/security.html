🔐 Security & Privacy

How LogHue keeps your data safe

Data Security

🗄️ Secure Storage

Your data is stored using Supabase, a trusted cloud database service. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest.

🔒 Authentication

We use secure session-based authentication. Your password is never stored in plain text — we use industry-standard hashing.

👥 Access Control

Your workspace content is only visible to invited members. Row-level security ensures data is isolated between workspaces.

Privacy

  • We never sell your data — Your information stays yours
  • Minimal data collection — We only collect what's needed to provide the service
  • Workspace privacy — Only invited members can see your workspace content
  • No third-party sharing — Your data is never shared with advertisers

Account Security

You can take additional steps to secure your account:

  • Use a strong, unique password
  • Keep your login credentials private
  • Log out when using shared devices

Cookies

LogHue uses cookies for essential functionality:

  • Session cookies — Keep you logged in
  • Preferences — Remember your theme settings

You can customize cookie preferences or decline non-essential cookies through the cookies banner in the app.

Account Deletion

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

When you delete your account:

  • All your personal data is removed
  • All tasks, logs, discussions and othere workspace related activities are anonymised
  • You are removed from all member workspaces

To delete your account: Go to Settings → Danger Zone → Delete Account

Questions?

If you have questions about security or privacy:

  • Email us at support@loghue.com
  • Read our Privacy Policy
