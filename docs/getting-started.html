🚀 Getting Started

Learn how to set up your account and start using LogHue

Create Your Account

Signing up for LogHue is quick and easy. Visit our signup page and choose how you want to register:

  • Email & Password — Create an account with your email address
  • Google — Sign in with your Google account
  • GitHub — Use your GitHub account for authentication

After verification, you'll be taken to your personal dashboard where you can create your first workspace.

Create a Workspace

Workspaces are the heart of LogHue — they're where your team collaborates. Here's how to create one:

1 Click "New Workspace"

On your dashboard, click the "+" button or "New Workspace" button.

2 Name Your Workspace

Give your workspace a clear, descriptive name that identifies the project or team.

3 Add a Description (Optional)

Write a brief description to help team members understand the workspace purpose.

That's it! You can create unlimited workspaces for different projects, teams, or departments.

Invite Team Members

Collaboration is better with others. To invite members to your workspace:

  • Go to your workspace and click Settings
  • Click Add Member
  • Enter the member's email address
  • Choose their role (Admin or Member)
  • Click "Send Invite"

Invited members will receive an email and can join the workspace right away.

Start Working

Now you're ready to go! Here's a quick overview of what you can do:

  • Create Tasks — Break down work into manageable tasks
  • Log Progress — Record what you've done each day
  • Discuss — Start conversations with your team
  • Take Notes — Keep important information handy
  • Archive — Keep completed work organized

Use the sidebar to navigate between different sections of your workspace.
