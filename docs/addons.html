Add‑ons

Extend LogHue with optional tools that enhance your workflow.

What Are Add‑ons?

Add‑ons are optional tools that expand what LogHue can do. They are not required for everyday use, but they provide extra capabilities for teams that want more automation, more power, or more specialized workflows.

Add‑ons integrate seamlessly with the LogHue interface and follow the same design principles: simple, fast, and distraction‑free.

ScanHue

ScanHue is the first official LogHue add‑on. It allows you to extract text from images using OCR (Optical Character Recognition). Whether you have handwritten notes, whiteboard photos, or printed documents, ScanHue converts them into clean, editable text.

What You Can Do With ScanHue

  • Upload an image and extract text instantly
  • Copy the extracted text with one click
  • Send the text directly into your notes

How to Use ScanHue

1 Open ScanHue

In the LogHue sidebar, click ScanHue or go to https://app.loghue.com/ScanHue. The tool opens in a clean, focused interface.

2 Upload an Image

Select the image you wish to scan file from your device.

3 Extract Text

Click Extract Text. ScanHue processes the image and displays the extracted text in seconds.

5 Use the Result

Copy the text or send it directly into your notes by clicking Edit in Notes.

Future Add‑ons

ScanHue is the first add‑on, but it won’t necessarily be the last. Additional add‑ons may be released over time depending on user needs, demand, and the direction of LogHue’s development.

Some add‑ons may be free, while others may require a paid plan. Details will be announced as new tools become available.
