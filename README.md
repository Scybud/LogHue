# LogHue

**Calm workspace management for modern teams.**

LogHue helps teams see what is happening without chasing updates.  
It brings tasks, logs, and progress into one focused workspace so work stays clear from start to finish.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Most teams don’t lack tools — they lack clarity.

Work gets scattered across chats, updates are missed, and progress becomes hard to follow.

**LogHue solves this by structuring how work flows:**
- Tasks are clearly assigned  
- Work is logged as it happens  
- Progress is visible without noise  
- Admins guide without constant interruptions  

---

## ✨ Core Features

### 🧩 Workspace-Centered Collaboration
Create dedicated workspaces for teams, projects, or departments.  
Keep tasks, logs, and members in one place.

### 📌 Task and Progress Tracking
Assign tasks with clear intent and track their state:
- In progress  
- Completed  
- Closed  

### 📝 Work Logging
Team members log what they did with:
- Notes  
- Dates  
- Progress updates  

No more guessing what’s been done.

### 👥 Clear Roles
- **Admins** review, guide, and close tasks  
- **Members** focus on doing and logging work  

Everyone sees what matters to them.

### 🔍 Review and Feedback
Admins can review logs, leave comments, and guide the next steps without constant meetings or messages.

### 🌙 Calm and Focused Design
A clean, dark interface designed to reduce noise and keep attention on real work.

---

## 🛠️ How It Works

1. Create a workspace  
2. Add your team  
3. Assign tasks  
4. Log work as it happens  
5. Review and guide progress  
6. Close tasks with clarity  

---

## 🎯 Who It’s For

- **Growing teams** that need structure  
- **Managers** who want visibility without micromanaging  
- **Team members** who want clarity in their work  
- **Small groups** tired of scattered communication  

---

## 💡 Example Workflow

A team creates a workspace for a project.  
A task is assigned.  
A member logs what they worked on and marks progress.  
An admin reviews the log and leaves feedback.  
Once complete, the task is closed.

Everything is clear. Nothing is lost.
